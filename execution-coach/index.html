The execution framework

Every goal goes through the same seven steps.

No exceptions, no shortcuts. This is what runs underneath every conversation.

01

Determine the objective

Vague goals get rejected. The coach won't move forward until the goal is concrete.

02

Determine the real obstacle

Separates genuine constraints from avoidance dressed up as a reason.

03

Create a practical action plan

A sequence of concrete tasks, each with a deadline and a measurable outcome.

04

Identify a 5–15 minute first task

Overwhelm gets broken down until the next step is small enough to start right now.

05

Require commitment

You say when you'll do it. The coach holds you to the time you gave.

06

Request proof of completion

Progress is reported, not assumed. Streaks and misses are both tracked.

07

Assign the next action immediately

No conversation ends without a next, executable step already on the table.
